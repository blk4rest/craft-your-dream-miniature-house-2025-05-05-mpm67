About Kitzey

Founded in 2020 by a collective of passionate miniature artists and craftsmen, Kitzey emerged from a shared vision to make the art of miniature house creation accessible to everyone. What started as a small online community has grown into a global platform, connecting creators from over 50 countries.

Our mission is to nurture creativity and craftsmanship through detailed guidance, community support, and innovative resources. We believe in the power of miniature art to bring joy, develop patience, and create lasting memories. Our core values of excellence, inclusivity, and sustainable crafting guide everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do I need to start?

For beginners, we recommend starting with a basic kit that includes wood pieces, adhesives, basic tools, and decorative elements. Our comprehensive starter guide provides a detailed list of essential materials and their estimated costs.

How long does it take to complete a project?

A typical miniature house project takes 20-40 hours to complete, depending on complexity and detail level. We recommend setting aside 2-3 hours per session and working at your own pace to ensure the best results.

Do I need any special skills or experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our guides are designed for all skill levels, starting with basic techniques and gradually introducing more advanced concepts as you progress.

Can I customize the designs?

Absolutely! While we provide detailed base plans, we encourage creativity and personalization. Our advanced guides include tips for customizing layouts, color schemes, and decorative elements.

What support is available if I get stuck?

Our community forum is available 24/7, where experienced crafters and our team can help with specific questions. We also offer weekly live Q&A sessions and detailed troubleshooting guides.
